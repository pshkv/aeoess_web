About Allset

Allset was a food ordering platform designed for local pickup, providing a seamless, cost-effective dining experience that allowed both consumers and restaurants to bypass the high fees charged by delivery apps like DoorDash and Uber Eats.

Founded in 2015 by Ukrainian founders Stas Matviyenko and Anna Polishchuk, Allset was backed by Andreessen Horowitz and raised through Series A. During my time there, I scaled the restaurant footprint from 300 to 3,400+ locations across 14 cities.

In June 2024, Allset was acquired by SoundHound AI (Nasdaq: SOUN) to accelerate their vision of voice-enabled food ordering across cars, TVs, and smart devices.

What I Learned

  • When the existing playbook doesn't work for you, build your own
  • Constraints (limited English, no experience) force creative solutions
  • Marketplaces fail when you sign supply faster than you can activate demand
  • The right "legend" (story/framing) matters more than volume of outreach
  • Retention problems are often onboarding + activation problems in disguise
  • B2B segments can save a struggling B2C business model
  • Rapid promotion comes from solving problems nobody asked you to solve
